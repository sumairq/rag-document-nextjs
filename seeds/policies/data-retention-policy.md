# Data Retention Policy

_Effective January 1, 2026._

## Purpose
This policy defines how long different categories of data are retained and when
they are deleted, to balance operational needs with privacy obligations.

## Retention periods
- **Customer account data**: retained for the life of the account, then deleted
  90 days after account closure.
- **Support tickets**: retained for 3 years from the date of resolution.
- **Application logs**: retained for 30 days, then automatically purged.
- **Financial records**: retained for 7 years to meet tax and audit
  requirements.
- **Marketing email lists**: contacts who do not engage for 24 consecutive
  months are removed.

## Deletion requests
Customers may request deletion of their personal data at any time. Verified
requests are fulfilled within 30 days, except where retention is legally
required (for example, financial records). Backups are purged on a rolling
90-day cycle, so deleted data may persist in backups for up to 90 days.

## Responsibilities
The Data Protection Officer reviews this policy annually. Engineering owns the
automated purging jobs and must verify quarterly that they run successfully.
