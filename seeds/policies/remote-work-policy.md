# Remote Work Policy

_Effective January 1, 2026. Applies to all full-time employees._

## Eligibility
Employees who have completed their 90-day onboarding period are eligible to
work remotely up to three days per week. Fully remote arrangements require
director approval and are reviewed annually.

## Core hours
Regardless of location, employees must be available during core hours of
10:00–15:00 in their registered time zone. Meetings should be scheduled within
core hours whenever possible.

## Equipment and stipend
The company provides a laptop and will reimburse up to $500 for home-office
equipment in the first year, and up to $150 annually thereafter. Receipts must
be submitted within 30 days of purchase through the expense portal.

## Security requirements
Remote employees must connect through the company VPN when accessing internal
systems, keep full-disk encryption enabled, and never store customer data on
personal devices. Lost or stolen equipment must be reported to IT within 24
hours.

## Expenses
Home internet is not reimbursed. Co-working space memberships may be reimbursed
up to $200 per month with manager approval.
